See tips for dating

I believe all of humanity can find love if they are looking in the right places.

When on the look for your lovely him or her, you must learn that optimistic patience is key.

A few tips from yours truly (me), who found his her earlier this year, are as follows:

  • Don't think you're too good for anyone
  • Don't think anyone is too good for you
  • Stereotypes only exist if you let them
  • Friends' opinions don't matter as much as you think
  • Be true to yourself

Oftentimes, we focus too much on what we think the other person should be like to best suite our needs. It would do us well if we began to focus more on what we can change in ourselves to be better suited for the person we end up dating.

Lastly, don't forget to

Smile, and enjoy the ride
